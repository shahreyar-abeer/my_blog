Making of the website: A tribute

Zauad Shahreer Abeer / 2020-09-16

  • The first mention should and always be the blogdown book, blogdown: Creating Websites with R Markdown
    Everyone who wants to make a website should start from here.
Having started with practically zero knowledge of CSS & HTML and stuff, these resources helped me along the way, showed the path.

  • After that, the person who should be thanked is Yihui Xie, the author of the book. The book as well as his blog serves as great resources. I don’t know how many times I had taken a peek at Yihui’s website while making this one. That blog is an inspiring one I must say.

  • This website is based on a theme by Yihui, Hugo Ivy

  • Leo’s website based also on Hugo Ivy, gave me some inspirations along the way.

  • I also stumbled upon Will Chase’s Glamour of Graphics talk at rstudio::conf 2020. This is where I learned about Butterick’s Practical Typography, a very nice read on modern typography and stuff. The Concourse font by Matthew Butterick is a gem.

  • Part of the design is taken from Live Free or Dichotomize.
    Although a lot of the elements are taken from it, it goes nowhere near its original class and style.

  • Finally, I’m grateful to Rbind Support for the free domain.
    And also to Netlify for the free service.
